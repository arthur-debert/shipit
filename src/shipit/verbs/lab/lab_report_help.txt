shipit lab report

`shipit lab report CELL` renders a cell's convergence curve from banked
review-round records.

CELL is either an id under `lab/cells/` or a path to a cell file. Reporting
loads the cell, its baseline cell when applicable, the ground-truth fixture,
and the local review-round record stores for each pinned repository.

It compares the cell against its baseline at equal budget and reports
cumulative major-or-worse recall, false positives or precision, token cost, and
latency per sweep point. Reporting is deterministic and token-free, so it is
safe to rerun while developing review strategy.
