`shipit spawn subagent` creates a Tree and launches a backend-agent Run.

Standalone issue implementer runs are cut from `origin/main`; epic workstream
implementer runs are cut from the epic umbrella branch and target that branch.
The implementer works in its Tree, opens a draft PR, and reports linkage for the
coordinator to drive.

The role is validated through the fixed Role Profile registry before any Tree is
provisioned or backend launched. Implementers get new write Trees and a draft-PR
result channel; shepherds attach to an existing PR write Tree; reviewers get a
shared read-only Tree and a posted-review result channel. Detached explorer or
coordinator launches are refused at preflight.
