`shipit channel` contains the Artifact channel's consumer-facing actions.

The Artifact channel lets a repo consume another repo's released build
artifacts as ordinary, version-pinned pixi dependencies. A repo declares the
pin in `.shipit.toml [artifact-deps]`, and `shipit install` projects it into a
managed pixi block so pixi resolves, locks, and fetches it like any dependency.

When the producing repo publishes a release, its fan-out cascades a version
bump here — see `shipit channel receive`.
